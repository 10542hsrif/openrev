1. Why OpenRev Reviewers?

1. Bleeding-Edge Rolling Release

OpenRev focuses more on rolling releases instead of stability, mainly because all reviewers should meet the reader's needs in their examination.

2. Open-Source

OpenRev is open-source in nature, so it is better!

Being open-source is our priority, mainly because you can edit or correct the mistaken thought or fact updates. You can make your own customized OpenRev Reviewers according to your needs and wants, without asking us or being sued. It will also help you to build your own custom reviewer from scratch.

3. Modernity

Hiring a tutor won't help you with your exams. They just give you their handwritten, or sometimes printed text-only reviewers at a large cost. Here at OpenRev, we equip our reviewers with the most modern technologies available for easier review. We are using our utmost efforts to make every things that you review clearer and easy to memorize, for free.

4. Lightweight and Easy to use

4. Lightweight and Easy to use You do not need to download our reviewers, because we also have a "Read it online" feature. It means that even if you are out of space, you can still read and memorize the items that you will answer on your examination. But if you cannot read it online, because of limited access to the internet, no problem! You can still download it.
